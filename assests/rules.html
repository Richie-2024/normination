Event Rules

Please ensure you adhere to the following rules and guidelines for a smooth and enjoyable event:

  • All nominees must adhere to the UG 256 Rules and Regulations of Conduct regarding the use of drugs and substances.
  • Voting for performance-based acts will be conducted at the venue by a panel of industry-level professionals, with audience participation.
  • Any form of cheating, syndicate activity, or plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Participants under the age of 18 must carry a fully signed parental consent form or be accompanied by their parents/guardians.
  • The categories "Socialite of the Year" and "Best Entertainment Blogger" will be judged based on social media following and activity.
  • Participation is open to all East African Community members, and attendance is open to everyone.
  • Nominations open on 20th November, and online voting begins on 1st December.
  • Early nominations provide an advantage to participants.
